About

My name is Miroslav Smukov, I’m from Serbia, and I’ve been a professional software developer since 2010. I joined the Salesforce community back in 2015 and kept it as my primary focus ever since. However, I do like to stray away from it from time to time and look into other technologies and platforms. If you want to know more about my past you can check out my LinkedIn profile.

On this blog, I’m going to share some of the more interesting problems and solutions I face during my career. I’m always open to any constructive critique, and I’m happy to alter my blog posts if I find your solutions to be better than mine.

Thank you and I hope you’ll find something useful and/or interesting for yourself on my blog.
